About the Show

Uncomfortable Topics. Real Conversations.

Ok to Be Offended is a podcast about real-life conversations and uncomfortable topics. We explore what offends us, what offends you, and how people with different backgrounds and opinions can still find common ground.

With guests from our neighborhood and beyond, the conversations are honest, meaningful… and somehow funny, mostly because we say the quiet part out loud.

We teach it is Ok to be offended. We hope we can offend you — but we have a secret weapon: we're stupid.
